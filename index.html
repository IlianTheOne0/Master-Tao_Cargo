Ready to cooperate?

Register your cargo!

  • To start cooperation, you need to register the cargo by providing information about the contents of the cargo, its value, and quantity. Also, attach photos to the application.
  • If you need to check the goods for quantity compliance, please indicate this in the "Note" field when registering the cargo on our website. This service is paid and costs 3 yuan per item in the order. Any additional checks of the cargo are discussed individually, including their cost.
  • Before sending the cargo to our warehouse in China, be sure to take care of labeling each outer box with your order number on our website so that we can identify your cargo. If you cannot do this yourself, ask your supplier to do it.
  • After sending the cargo to our warehouse in China, you must provide the tracking number for tracking parcels in China for each item in the order. You can enter the tracking number on the order page. The tracking number is another measure to identify your cargo in our warehouse.
  • If you have registered the cargo according to our instructions and did everything correctly, the status of your order will change to "At the warehouse" upon receipt of your cargo at the warehouse in China.
